About us

We are a small community of people who live on narrow boats at New Islington Marina. We formed NIMCIC so that we can do great things in and around our community to the benefit of visitors and residents alike!

Our Aims

To fully manage the area known as New Islington Marina.
Participate in the development of projects that will support new and existing businesses in New Islington Marina and the surrounding Ancoats area.

All income generated will be used to invest in further projects for the benefit of the local community

Managing Our Space


The residents of New Islington Marina would like to manage the space where we live. We have substantial knowledge of the marina and all of it’s inhabitants whether they be animal or human. We know our space better than anyone, therefore we are the best-placed people to take the best care of it and make it better for the local people and visitors.

Our Wild Neighbours and Friends


NIMIC’s first real project is very much on-going. Since 2018, we noticed a substantial decline in the Wildlife in and around the Marina. We recognise that City Wildlife is important for community mental health so we strive to regenerate the park back to it’s former glory days when it teemed with wildlife.
After seeing 2 years of unsuccessful and miserable breeding seasons, in spring 2020, NIMCIC took the initiative to give the wildlife a little helping hand. We gathered nesting materials and fixed up the old floating nests and made them safer in the hope our friends on the water would build strong and healthy nests – they did!
So far, Spring 2020 has been a phenomenal success.
